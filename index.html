About Kids Next Door

KND revolves around a group of an aspiring artists using artworks as NFTs. Each member has their own unique art style, capable of creating Multi-media projects such as Traditional drawings, Graphic designs, Digital Photography, and Oil Paintings.

- KND Anonymous

Our Services

Digital Photography

Explore the most important functions and techniques used to create digital photographs with your digital camera.

Graphic Design

Explore graphic communication through the understanding of the elements and principles of design as well as the design process.

Freehand Drawing

Organize yourself around the concepts for drawing from perception. It is based on working from still life, architecture, landscape.

Painting

Learn about a wide range of painting styles and techniques to help you create better portraits, landscapes, abstract.

Typography and Layout

Provides thorough investigation in the readability and legibility of type, as well as effective typographic usages in daily life applications.

Animation

Learn about storyboarding, character development, scriptwriting, and cinematography, as well as industry-standard processes and specialist skills.
